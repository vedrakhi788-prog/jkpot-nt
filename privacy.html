Privacy Statement

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Data Handling Philosophy

QuizMania is an engagement platform for everyone. We don't ask for registered profiles and we're dedicated to protecting information by gathering only what's necessary.

Information We Handle

  • Local data: game activity is stored temporarily in your browser’s localStorage to track recent sessions. This data is never sent to our servers and is not personally identifiable.
  • Contact messages: if you (the parent) choose to contact us via our form, we receive the information you submit to respond to your inquiry.

Tracking Tools and Promotions

We don't employ tracking tools. The complete experience runs within your device and contains no advertisements to protect your enjoyment.

Your Options

  • You can delete your browser's cached data at any moment to remove any locally kept activity.
  • Using your device's private or incognito feature will stop session details from being retained.

Contact Information

Have questions? You can use the messaging feature on our main site to contact us.
